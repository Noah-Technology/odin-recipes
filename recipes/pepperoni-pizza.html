Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pizza sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Roll out the pizza dough.
  2. Cover dough with pizza sauce.
  3. Cover with cheddar cheese.
  4. Cover with mozzarella cheese.
  5. Cover with pepperoni.
  6. Bake at 450 degrees for 10 mins.
  7. Season with italian seasoning.
  8. Enjoy!
