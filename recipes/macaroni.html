macaroni

Description

Ingredients

  • Macaroni pasta
  • Sharp cheddar cheese
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Smoked paprika
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder

    • Steps

    1. Cook your pasta to al dente.
    2. Place your butter into the pot.
    3. Place your flour into the pot.
    4. Add milk
    5. Season with different seasonings to taste.
    6. Add in your pasta.
    7. Mix
    8. Enjoy!
