Sandwich

Description

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Sliced cheddar cheese
  • Ham slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Toast your bread.
  2. Cover insides of bread in mayo.
  3. Place ham slices in sandwich.
  4. Place cheese slice in sandwich.
  5. Place lettuce in sandwich.
  6. Place tomato in sandwich.
  7. Cut in halves. (if preferred!)
  8. Enjoy!
